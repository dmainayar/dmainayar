## Current Projects

### Lex Core

- 📖 **Lex Reader** - the main reading platform: public-domain library, uploads, shelves, progress tracking, highlights, notes, social discovery, and author pages.
- 🎧 **Audiobooks** - 30k+ LibriVox titles, synced playback, chapter mapping, full-screen player, mini-player, CarPlay-style audio metadata, and native mobile audio work.
- 🤖 **Lex AI** - reading companion, inline edits, summaries, book intelligence, recommendations, and context-aware chat over a user's library.
- 🌍 **Translations** - AI-assisted translations for classic works, public-domain editions, and Lex-original releases.
- 📄 **PDF to EPUB** - scanned books and PDFs converted into reflowable reading experiences.
- 🗺️ **Literary Map** - bookstores, libraries, literary events, and discovery around real-world reading culture.
- 🧑‍💻 **Creator Tools** - publisher onboarding, 90% author royalties, uploads, premium editions, and book production workflows.

### Reading Experiments

- ⚡ **BlitzReads** - "read at the speed of thought"; rapid serial visual presentation plus sentence-context speed reading.
- 🏆 **BookTierList** - ranking books visually, because taste is more fun when it starts arguments.
- 🇺🇸 **POTUSTierList** - presidents, tiers, history nerdery, and exactly the kind of discourse the internet deserves.
- 📚 **Comprehensive Summaries** - Blinkist-style Lex editions for major works, generated from actual book text and published as readable books.
- 🔎 **X-Ray / Book Intelligence** - characters, chapters, themes, and reading context without fake quote theater.

### Knowledge & Citations

- 🧾 **Lexipedia** - a live Wikipedia mirror that makes book citations useful instead of dead footnotes.
- 🔖 **Citation Resolver** - detects book refs, parses ISBN/title/author/page, and opens the source in Google Books or Lex.
- 🧠 **Book Graph** - editions, translations, audiobooks, PDFs, IA scans, Gutenberg records, Open Library, Google Books, Apple Books, Hardcover, and more.
- 🪪 **Author/Bio Pipelines** - catalog enrichment, author pages, summaries, covers, and metadata cleanup at scale.

### Voice, Audio & Media

- 🎙️ **LexVox** - voice generation for long-form content, designed around books instead of 15-second clips.
- 🗣️ **Audiobook Generation** - Qwen3-TTS experiments, voice design, voice cloning, chapter extraction, batch generation, and S3 delivery.
- 🎼 **Ambient Reading Audio** - music manifests, background tracks, reading modes, and audio engine work.
- 🎬 **Empire-Builders** - produced, edited, and presented history content; Roman roads, empires, biographies, presidents, and long-form educational videos.

### History & Publishing

- 🏛️ **Empire-Builders Podcast** - 24+ episodes on imperial civilizations, late Rome, Carolingians, Holy Roman Empire, and the deep roots of state formation.
- 📜 **Empire-Builders Book Club** - biographies, classics, and primary sources for people who think bookstores should come with battle maps.
- ✍️ **Little Corporal's Dispatch** - history, econ, geopolitics, tech, books, movies, and occasional Napoleonposting.
- 🏗️ **Library of Alexandria Project** - the larger mission: make the world's serious books easier to find, read, cite, hear, summarize, translate, and share.

## What I'm Building Toward

- 📚 **A real alternative to Amazon for books** - better economics for authors, better UX for readers, no lock-in.
- 🌐 **A universal reading layer** - EPUBs, PDFs, scans, audiobooks, citations, translations, summaries, and social context in one place.
- 🧠 **AI that helps people read more deeply** - not fake homework summaries, but tools for comprehension, memory, and discovery.
- 🏛️ **History media with receipts** - videos, podcasts, and essays connected back to sources and books.
- 🛠️ **Many small tools** - because a good side project is just a future feature trying to escape.
